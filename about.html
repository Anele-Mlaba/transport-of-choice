About Us

Our Story

Transport of Choice was built from a simple idea and a lot of determination.

When the traditional varsity route didn’t work out, Nhlalonhle chose not to give up or limit himself. Instead of putting all his eggs in one basket, he decided to create his own opportunity with the support of his brothers. What began as a small scholar transport service slowly grew into something more.

Through consistency, learning along the way, and using social media to reach people, Transport of Choice developed into a service focused on reliability and everyday transport needs. The journey hasn’t been about shortcuts or overnight wins, but about steady progress and doing things properly.

Today, Transport of Choice continues to grow step by step, built on real experiences, community support, and a commitment to showing up when people need transport they can depend on.
