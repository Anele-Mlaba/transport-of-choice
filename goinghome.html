Going Home Package

The Going Home Package by Transport of Choice is made for students who want a smart, affordable way to get home during semester breaks. We match you with 3–6 students going the same direction and you carpool home together. Easy. Safe. Affordable.

How It Works

Drop Your Details

You tell us:

  1. Where you’re studying
  2. Where you’re going home

That’s it.

We Find Your Travel Squad

As soon as 3–6 students are heading the same way:

  1. We create a travel group
  2. Connect everyone going that direction
  3. Lock in the trip

Ride Together

On travel day:

  1. One ride
  2. Multiple drop-offs along the route
  3. No stress, no drama
